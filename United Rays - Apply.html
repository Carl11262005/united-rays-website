Dear Prospective Member,

Thank you for your interest in the United Rays Corvette Club of Pittsburgh, PA Inc. — one of the premier automobile clubs in the city of Pittsburgh. It is our prayer that as you explore what our club has to offer, you will come to know a family united by the pride of Corvette ownership, fellowship, and a love for the open road.

Since our founding in June 1977, the United Rays have been committed to preserving the Corvette as America’s true sports car while promoting the enthusiasm, camaraderie, and fellowship that comes with ownership. We are also deeply committed to the city of Pittsburgh — hosting philanthropic, civic, and community events throughout the region.

Membership in the United Rays is a privilege extended by invitation. To be considered, you must be sponsored by a current Full or Associate member of the club. Completed applications are reviewed by the Executive Board within 45 days. Upon acceptance, you will be expected to uphold the Constitution, By-Laws, and Operating Procedures of the United Rays Corvette Club.

We would be excited to welcome you to one of our cruises during the season — May through September — and hope to add you to our family. If you have any questions before or after submitting your application, please do not hesitate to reach out.

Rolling with pride,

Kim Young

President · United Rays Corvette Club of Pittsburgh, PA Inc.
